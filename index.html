What is BOS (Break of Structure)?

Bullish BOS: When the candle crosses above AND closes above a previous swing high, confirming strong upward momentum and trend continuation.

Bearish BOS: When the candle crosses below AND closes below a previous swing low, confirming strong downward momentum and trend continuation.

BOS indicates the trend is continuing in the same direction.

What is CHOCH (Change of Character)?

Bullish CHOCH: In a downtrend, when the candle crosses above AND closes above a previous swing high, signaling potential trend reversal to uptrend.

Bearish CHOCH: In an uptrend, when the candle crosses below AND closes below a previous swing low, signaling potential trend reversal to downtrend.

CHOCH indicates the market character is changing and potential trend reversal.

Note: Patterns require price to CROSS the level AND CLOSE beyond it for stronger confirmation. Works on all timeframes (Daily, Weekly, Monthly).

## Why Nifty 50?

The Nifty 50 represents:
- **Top 50 companies** by market capitalization on NSE
- **~66% of the free float** market capitalization of NSE
- **13 sectors** of the Indian economy
- **Highly liquid** stocks with good trading volumes
- **Blue chip** stocks with strong fundamentals
- **Benchmark index** widely tracked by investors

## Pattern Detection

Each of these 50 stocks is analyzed for:
- **BOS (Break of Structure)** - Trend continuation patterns
- **CHOCH (Change of Character)** - Trend reversal patterns
- **Date Range Filtering** - Historical pattern analysis
- **Closing Price Based** - Strong confirmation signals

## Scan Time

- **Single Date Scan**: ~50-90 seconds (all 50 stocks)
- **Weekly Scan (7 days)**: ~6-10 minutes
- **Monthly Scan (30 days)**: ~25-35 minutes

The time varies based on:
- Network speed
- Yahoo Finance API response time
- Date range selected
- Number of patterns detected

## Note on Nifty 50 Changes

The Nifty 50 composition is reviewed **semi-annually** (twice a year) by NSE. Stocks may be added or removed based on:
- Market capitalization changes
- Liquidity requirements
- Sector representation
- Corporate actions

This list is current as of **February 2026**. For the latest official Nifty 50 composition, visit the NSE India website.
